{ When do you pay? }

Each expert sets their own rate.

After submitting your brief, we suggest 3 experts in your budget. The suggestions may or may not be the same price. When you choose which expert to work with, we calculate the cost & ask you to pay.

{ Privacy options }

Open source, Private or NDA?

We record session so you can refer to them again. Recordings also help our experts build credibility.

Open source: we may use your session in the public sphere for educational purposes.

Private: only you have access to the recording.

NDA: we take your non-disclosure agreement & find experts that will sign it.
