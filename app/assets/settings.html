Payment Settings

We're currently experimenting with different payment providers (paypal+stripe). We've had requests for bitcoin options and others. If you have a strong preference for other providers, let us know.

Our expert payout policy is to pay on completion of the chunks you've been booked. I.e. if you're booked for a 1 hour call - we will pay you after the call. If you've been booked for 5 hours, we will pay you on completion of your 5 hours. Paying out in blocks allows us to minimize the payment collection fees you pay to the payment providers. It also reduces our administration overhead.
