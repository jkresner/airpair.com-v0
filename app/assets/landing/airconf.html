Keynote Panels

The Future of Consulting

With remote work and distributed teams becoming more popular each year, what does the future hold for consulting? Consultancies like Thoughtworks and Pivotal Labs have gone beyond outsourcing. The best consultancies leave clients with new skills on top of deliverables. But will this be enough going forward, what does the future hold? Obie Fernandez – Hashrocket , CEO (prev) | AirPair, CTO
Mike Mason – ThoughtWorks, Head of Technology
Evan Henshaw-Plath – Neo, CTO
Chad Pytel – ThoughtBot, CEO

Will Pairing Ever Be Mainstream?

Does pairing make sense for other disciplines outside of software. What makes pairing a compelling value proposition? Desc coming soon.
Rob Mee – Pivotal Labs , CEO

Software Engineering vs Entrepreneurship

Software Engineering is a precise discipline. Often early in a company's lifetime, thing needs to be scrappy and fast. Are Software Engineering and Entrepreneurship at odds? Is there a way to have both quality and speed, or can the two only exist at different stages of every successful software project? Jonathon Kresner – AirPair, CEO
Deep Nishar – LinkedIn, SVP Product/UX
Charlie Cheever – Quora, Cofounder

Developer Brand Marketing

What are the best strategies for building a personal brand as a developer? Should you work on your reputation score on StackOverflow, publish open source projects on Github, write on your personal blog, or speak at meetups and conferences? Our panelists will discuss their experiences and will share tips and tricks for establishing and managing a reputation in the developer community.
Igor Lebovic – AirPair, CMO
Garry Tan – YCombinator, Partner
