Do I Need a Mentor?

Learning any technical subject has a learning curve. For a self-directed student, it can be daunting to begin wrapping his or her brain around a large new body of knowledge.

There are plenty of great resources available for the vast majority of technical subjects, from online tutorials, through user forums, to printed books.

What our customers tell us is that this approach works, but has several drawbacks:

  • New concepts could be hard to grasp by learning them through static text in a book
  • It's easy to get stuck in a rat hole, spending hours or even days on trivial problems
  • When working on practice projects, there is no exposure to common patterns or best practices. There is no feedback loop
  • At the end of the day, there'a gap between someone who read a book on a subject and built their first app and an engineer who is ready to ship production quality code

An Airpair mentor is a complement to books and tutorials that addresses all of the above concerns. You are paired up with a technical subject matter expert that you work with one-on-one via video chat, on your schedule.
