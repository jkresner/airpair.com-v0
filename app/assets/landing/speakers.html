Keynote Panels

The Future of Consulting

With remote work and distributed teams becoming more popular each year, what does the future hold for consulting? Consultancies like Thoughtworks and Pivotal Labs have gone beyond outsourcing. The best consultancies leave clients with new skills on top of deliverables. But will this be enough going forward, what does the future hold? Obie Fernandez – AirPair, CTO
Martin Fowler – ThoughtWorks, Chief Scientist
Evan Henshaw-Plath – Neo, CTO

Will Pairing Ever Be Mainstream?

When should you pair? This panel will explore why Pair Programming came about and address its cost effectiveness in the context of why it was created? Tune in to the founders of some of the most progressive software shops discussing the tricks and pitfalls of managing teams that embrace pairing.
Kent Beck – eXtreme Programing, Creator
Rob Mee – Pivotal Labs, CEO

Can you still fake SEO?

Google gets smarter every year. At first you could hide elements with CSS and stuff what you wanted in meta tags. Now google is smart enough to execute javascript to make sure there's no trickier. What else does google do that renders tactics useless and what can we do to generate great content and great SEO with google's ever increasing intelligence. Igor Lebovic – AirPair CMO

Software Engineering vs Entrepreneurship

Software Engineering is precise or broken, yet Entrepreneurship especially early in company's lifetime often needs to be scrappy and out the door as fast as possible. Are the two really at odds? Is there a way to have both quality and speed, or do the two exist at different stages of every software project. Jonathon Kresner – AirPair, CEO
Charlie Cheever – Facebook Connect & Quora, Creator
