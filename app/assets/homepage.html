{ why I started airpair }

Jonathon Kresner: I started airpair to help developers.

I've ALWAYS loved coding product. When things run smooth & you're knocking out screens, life is grand. But often teams lose their way. Products get delayed and instead of knowing if your market assumptions are right, you're stuck battling technology. I know because I was there in both of my first startups. I needed access to mobile experts and db gurus, even though I consider myself a strong engineer.

airpair is for companies that want to get back on track, or better, start off on the right foot by periodically getting expert help to deliver faster.
